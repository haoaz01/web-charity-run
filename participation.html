Event Participation Information

How to Participate in Charity Run 2024

Participating in Charity Run 2024 is a wonderful opportunity to contribute to a meaningful cause in the community. Below is a detailed guide for each participant type:

1. Runners

  • Register to Participate: Visit the official Charity Run 2024 website and navigate to the "Registration" section. Fill in the required personal information and select the distance you wish to run.
  • Receive Your Bib Number: After registering, you will receive a confirmation email with details about the location and time for bib number collection.
  • Prepare Before the Event: Train regularly and ensure you have a proper meal before the event day.
  • Entry Fee: The registration fee is $30, which includes a race bib, a t-shirt, and a participation medal.

2. Volunteers

  • Sign Up as a Volunteer: Go to the "Volunteers" section on the website and fill out the registration form.
  • Volunteer Training: Receive detailed information about the training session for volunteers.
  • Event Day: Arrive at the event location early to prepare and receive your assigned tasks.
  • Incentives: Volunteers will receive a free t-shirt, refreshments during the event, and a certificate of appreciation for their contribution.

3. Sponsors

  • Become a Sponsor: Contact us via email for more information on sponsorship packages.
  • Brand Promotion: Sponsors will be promoted through various media channels and banners at the event.
  • Sponsorship Tiers:
    • Gold Sponsor - $5,000
    • Silver Sponsor - $3,000
    • Bronze Sponsor - $1,000

Important Rules

  • All participants must adhere to the event schedule and follow safety guidelines.
  • Wearing a bib number is mandatory during the race.
  • Participants should respect the venue and fellow participants.
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.
  • All entries are final, and no refunds will be issued for cancellations.

Pricing Information

The participation fees for different categories are as follows:

5K Run: $30

10K Run: $40

Half Marathon: $60

Fun Walk: $20

Children (under 12): $10
