Overview

Charity Run 2024 will be held to raise awareness and funds for communities affected by natural disasters, especially floods. Which not only provides an opportunity for people to participate in physical activities but also contributes to a noble cause. With a variety of running categories for all ages and fitness levels, along with exciting entertainment such as live music performances and food stalls, Charity Run 2024 promises to be a festival full of energy and solidarity. We call on everyone to participate, register online and together make a difference, contributing to building a brighter future for those in need.
